Donors Choose

DonorsChoose.org is a US based nonprofit organization that allows individuals to donate money directly to public school classroom projects. Public school teachers post classroom project requests on the platform, and individuals have the option to donate money directly to fund these projects. The classroom projects range from pencils and books to computers and other expensive equipments for classrooms.

DonorsChoose.org is making the platform data open and available for making discoveries and building applications. The data in the above visualizations is taken from the project dataset which contains information about all classroom projects that have been posted to the site.
